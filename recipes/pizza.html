Description

Pizza is a beloved dish of Italian origin, consisting of a round, flattened base of wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and various other ingredients such as meats, vegetables, and herbs, then baked at a high temperature, often in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni (optional)
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add cheese and toppings of your choice.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
  6. Slice and serve hot.
