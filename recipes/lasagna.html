Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with wide, flat pasta sheets layered with a rich tomato sauce, cheese (often including ricotta and mozzarella), and sometimes ground meat, all baked together in the oven, resulting in a hearty and layered pasta dish that is typically cut into square servings; the word "lasagna" can also refer to the type of wide pasta sheet used in the dish itself.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic and onions

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles.
  2. Cook the beef and mix with tomato sauce.
  3. Layer the noodles, sauce, and cheeses.
  4. Bake at 375Â°F for 45 minutes.
  5. Let it cool and serve.
