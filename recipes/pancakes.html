Description

Pancakes are a classic breakfast dish made from a batter of flour, eggs, milk, and butter, cooked on a griddle or frying pan and often served with syrup, fruit, or whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk eggs and milk, then combine with dry ingredients.
  3. Heat a pan and add butter.
  4. Pour batter into the pan and cook until bubbles form.
  5. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  6. Serve with syrup and toppings of choice.
